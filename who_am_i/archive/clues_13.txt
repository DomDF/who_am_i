Hidetoshi Nakata, Nakata
I scored 11 international goals in 77 caps, including goals in the FIFA World Cup and FIFA Confederations Cup
I was named as a global ambassador for special olympics in 2014
I was added as an icon player in FIFA 19
I have played in two Olympic Games and three FIFA World Cup tournaments
I have played alongside Adrian Mutu, Jay-Jay Okocha, and Shunsuke Nakamura
I am a Serie A and Coppa Italia winner, and was made a knight of the order of the star of Italian Solidarity in 2005
I featured in the famous ‘secret tournament (scorpion KO)’ Nike advert in the build up to the 2002 Fifa World Cup
I have also played alongside Francesco Totti, Adriano, and Yasuhito Endo
I developed my own line of sake and created a mobile education app, ‘Sakenomy’ in 2015
I was nominated for both the Ballon d’Or and FIFA World Player of the Year in 1998, 1999 and 2001, becoming the first player from my continent to be ever be nominated
