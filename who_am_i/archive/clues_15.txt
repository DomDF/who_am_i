Predrag Mijatović, Mijatović
I scored 27 goals in 73 international caps
I played alongside Gaizka Mendieta, Nuno Gomes, Davor Šuker, and Nemanja Vidic
I am a Coppa Italia and La Liga winner
I was voted as the La Liga Best Foreign Player in the 1995/96 season
I was the runner-up (behind Ronaldo) for the 1997 Ballon d'Or
My nation was eliminated in the knockout stages of both the 1998 FIFA World Cup and UEFA Euro 2000 by the Netherlands
I scored the (controversial) winning goal in a UEFA Champions League final
As director of football for Real Madrid, I signed Fabio Cannavaro, Ruud van Nistelrooy, and Marcelo
I have also played alongside Clarence Seedorf, Raúl González, Dejan Savicevic, and Vladimir Jugović
I was voted the FR Yugoslavia Footballer of the Year on three occasions (most recently in 1998)
