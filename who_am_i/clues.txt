Neville Southall, Southall
I was twice nominated for the Ballon d'Or
I have played alongside Ryan Giggs, Gary Lineker, and Andrey Kanchelskis
I am a European Cup Winner' Cup winner
I featured in World Soccer's 100 Greatest Players of the 20th Century
I am an FA Cup and FA Charity Shield winner
I have also played alongside Ian Rush, Dave Watson, and David Unsworth
My autobiography was titled, 'The Binman Chronicles' and was the sixth best-selling football book of 2012
I was BBC Wales Sports Personality of the Year in 1995
I hold multiple Everton club records including all-time record appearances and clean sheets
I famously told Michael Owen "well done, he's 13" after he celebrating scoring against a child on his soccer skills TV show
